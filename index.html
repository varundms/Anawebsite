Everything You Need in One Platform

Seven capabilities that turn your KPIs into results — not just reports.

Feature 01

Multiple Companies / Business Units Combined in One Platform

Running a conglomerate or multiple subsidiaries? ANA aggregates performance data from all your business units into a single consolidated view. Compare entities side-by-side, spot group-wide trends, and drill down into specific companies with one tap. No more manually combining Excel sheets from different offices. Your entire business empire, unified.

"Managing multiple entities shouldn't be a reporting nightmare. ANA gives you the group-level bird's-eye view while keeping every subsidiary's data just a click away."
01
Feature 02

Organisation Health Score

Every month, ANA looks at all your departments: Finance, Sales, HR, Operations and more then gives you a single score out of 100. Think of it like a health check for your entire business. You see at a glance whether things are on track (green), need attention (amber), or are critical (red). No spreadsheets. No guesswork. Just one clear answer every time you open the dashboard.

"Your board doesn't have time to read 60 KPI reports. With ANA, they don't have to. One score tells the whole story."
02
Feature 03

Health by Category

ANA breaks your company's performance down by department : Finance, Purchase, Sales, HR, Admin, and more. Each department gets its own performance score and colour, so you can immediately spot who's excelling and who needs intervention. You also see how each department has changed compared to last month. No more wading through department-wise Excel files. One screen tells you everything across the entire organisation.

"You run nine departments. ANA watches all of them and tells you exactly which one needs your time today."
03
Feature 04

Live Target vs. Actual Tracking

For every goal your business sets — whether it's monthly sales, customer complaints, or pending orders. ANA shows you the actual number versus the target, updated in real time. A green dot means you've achieved it. An amber or red dot means something needs fixing, right now. You don't wait for the month-end review to find out there's a problem, you know today.

"Stop finding out about problems after the damage is done. ANA tells you the moment a target is at risk, not five weeks later."
04
Feature 05

MIS Reporting Matrix

Every leader needs reports for board meetings, investor updates, and management reviews. With ANA, these reports build themselves. It pulls together trend charts for every KPI daily, weekly, or monthly and lets you export a polished deck as a PDF or image with one click. What used to take your data team 3 days now takes 3 seconds. You can even play it as a live presentation directly from the screen.

"Your MIS report used to be a weekend project. Now it's a one-click export formatted, accurate, and ready for the boardroom."
05
Feature 06

Closed-Loop Action Tracking

Most dashboards show you the problem and then leave you to sort it out yourself. ANA goes further. When a KPI is off track, you can immediately assign an action to the right person, set a deadline, and track whether it gets done. You see how many tasks are pending, in progress, overdue, or completed — all in one place. So problems don't just get spotted, they actually get solved.

"Other tools show you what's broken. ANA makes sure someone actually fixes it and proves it's done."
06
Feature 07

Zero-Entry Integration

Most companies already use software like SAP, Tally, Salesforce, or Zoho. ANA plugs directly into all of them so your data flows in automatically, every day, without anyone having to copy-paste or upload files. Your ERP keeps doing what it does. Your CRM keeps doing what it does. ANA sits above them, pulls it all together, and shows you the full picture. No manual data entry. No duplicate work. No new habits to form.

"You've already invested in SAP, Salesforce, and Tally. ANA makes that investment finally talk to each other automatically."
07

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Heyxia Ana.

How many KPIs can we track in Ana?

There's no hard limit. Ana is built to handle large KPI sets across multiple departments — you can configure KPIs per group with individual owners, frequencies, and targets.

Can different team members see only their department's data?

Yes. Ana supports role-based access so department heads see what's relevant to them, while leadership gets the full cross-organisation view.

What frequency options are available for KPI tracking?

Ana supports daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly KPI frequencies — all manageable from a single dashboard without separate setups for each.

What happens if data isn't entered on time?

Ana flags it as a missing KPI and marks it overdue. Automated email reminders are sent to the responsible team member based on your configured reminder frequency.

Can we export data from Ana?

Yes. You can export KPI data and corrective actions as CSV, and generate presentation-ready reports as PDF or images directly from the dashboard.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation is fast. Most organisations can set up their core KPIs and add team members in under a few hours.

Does Heyxia offer training?

Yes. We provide onboarding support and training to ensure your team is comfortable with the platform from day one.

Still have questions? Our team typically responds within a few hours.
